Cascade Lifting Work Checklist - CNG Station

A. Pre-Lifting Checks

  • Work Permit obtained
  • Site Safety Briefing conducted
  • Weather condition checked
  • Equipment certification verified
  • Load weight verified with crane capacity
  • Ground condition stable
  • Clearance area free of obstructions
  • PPE compliance verified
  • Fire extinguisher available
  • Cascade depressurized & purged

B. During-Lifting Checks

  • Qualified operators only
  • Communication signals assigned
  • Slow & steady load movement
  • Personnel exclusion zone maintained
  • Balanced load maintained
  • Tag lines used
  • No standing under suspended load

C. Post-Lifting Checks

  • Safe placement of cascade
  • Load securing checked
  • Equipment inspected post-use
  • Permit closed & signed off
  • Housekeeping done
  • Incident reporting completed
